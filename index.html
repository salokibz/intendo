Flagship project · Active

Reusable Sanitary Towels

Period poverty keeps girls out of school. Our reusable sanitary towel kits give six months of dignity per kit — and last up to three years. Hand-cut and assembled in our Kampala studio, distributed to schools and community centres in eastern Uganda and Kampala's informal settlements.

  • 500+ Girls served
  • 3 Years per kit
  • $10 Funds one kit
