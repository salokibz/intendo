# Itendo Foundation Website Proposal

## Coming Soon Page
A modern, elegant landing page for Itendo Foundation using the attached brand colors and logo.

### Key elements
- Hero message: "From fashion to impact. From creativity to community."
- Countdown to launch: 14 days with days, hours, minutes, seconds.
- Three impact pillars: Itendo (Impact), Fashion, Travel.
- Contact form that submits to `info@itendofoundation.org`.
- Visible contact details: email `info@itendofoundation.org` and phone `0774630904`.

## Suggested Main Menu
- Home
- What We Do
- Fund a Pack
- Impact Fashion
- Trips
- Real Stories
- Transparency
- Contact

## Homepage Structure
1. Hero section
   - Direct action. Real impact.
   - Clear CTA buttons: Contact Us, Explore Itendo.
   - Launch countdown.

2. What We Do
   - Reusable menstrual packs
   - Support vulnerable families
   - Impact fashion

3. Contact section
   - Email + phone
   - Contact form

## Internal Pages Outline
- **What We Do**: reusable menstrual packs, community support, impact fashion.
- **Fund a Pack**: donate per-pack support, automatic amount calculation.
- **Impact Fashion**: garments made in Uganda, 15–20% of sales fund the work.
- **Trips**: responsible travel for small groups, inside Uganda, transparent pricing.
- **Real Stories**: short stories with real photos and dignity.
- **Transparency**: clear use of funds, what is done and why.
- **Contact**: simple form, email, phone, social links.

## Notes for the Designer
- Use the logo file as `logo.png` in the same folder.
- Keep the page classic and polished, not generic.
- Use green and orange accents from the brand.
- Add subtle motion and soft glass-like layering.
- The form should ideally connect to the foundation email or a backend contact service.
